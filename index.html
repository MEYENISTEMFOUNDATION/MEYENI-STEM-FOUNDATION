Welcome to Meyeni STEM Foundation

Empowering girls in STEM education across Ghana

About Us

At Meyeni STEM Foundation, our mission is to inspire and equip young girls to pursue careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. We are passionate about making a difference in the field of STEM education. Our team consists of individuals from diverse backgrounds—engineers, scientists, educators, and innovators—who share a common goal: to empower the next generation of girls in Ghana to pursue careers in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. , Through our initiatives, we strive to inspire and equip girls with the skills and confidence they need to excel in STEM fields. Together, we are building a supportive network that fosters inclusivity, innovation, and leadership among future female leaders in Ghana. (STEM). We provide mentorship, hands-on workshops, and community outreach to help bridge the gender gap in these fields.

Our Programs

  • Mentorship Programs: Connecting girls with female STEM mentors. We are committed to "paying it forward" by sharing our knowledge and experiences with young women.
  • Hackathons/play and learn: Hands-on activities to spark interest in technology.
  • STEM Forums: Telling our STEM stories to spark inspire the next generation.
  • Community Outreach: Raising awareness about the importance of menstrual hygiene in STEM education.

Our Initiatives

We are committed to promoting STEM education for girls across Ghana and the African continent through our focused initiatives:

Savannah for STEM (S4S)

This initiative is focused on promoting STEM education for girls in the northern parts of Ghana. Our aim is to inspire and empower young girls in underrepresented regions to pursue careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

STEM She Can

Targeted at girls in the southern parts of Ghana, this initiative provides mentorship, resources, and workshops to encourage and support their STEM education journey.
